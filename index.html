Pochita is too cute and he is the best

Pochita (ポチタ?) is the Chainsaw Devil (チェンソーの悪魔 Chensō no Akuma?) who embodies the fear of chainsaws. He was the original Chainsaw Man (チェンソーマン Chensō Man?) prior to becoming Denji's heart. He is the overarching titular protagonist of the series.
